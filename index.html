Privacy Policy for Chatti

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to Chatti. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our core features, Chatti requires access to certain permissions:

  • Microphone Access: We use the speech_to_text plugin to convert your voice into text. This is essential for the app's functionality.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic technical data (OS version, device model) to ensure the app runs smoothly.

2. How We Use Audio Data

When you use the voice-to-text feature, your audio is processed as follows:

  • On-Device Processing: Where possible, speech recognition is performed directly on your device.
  • Third-Party Services: Depending on your device, audio data may be sent to Google (Android) or Apple (iOS) speech recognition services to convert it to text. This data is governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • No Storage: Chatti does not record, store, or upload your raw audio files to our own servers.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data processed through the app. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or mobile network is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Chatti is built using Flutter. Depending on your platform, please refer to:

  • Google Privacy Policy (Android)
  • Apple Privacy Policy (iOS)

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
